About Me

Hello! My name is Chedy and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2019 when I decided to create a fake League Of Legends login page to gain profit — turns out hacking taught me a lot about HTML & CSS!

Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of working with a French digital services company.

My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences.

On my spare time, I love to attend conferences to meet new people, build projects and do freelance work.

I'm comfortable working with both Front-End and Back-End technologies.

As a Full-Stack Developer, I can do the Front-End and Back-End code, work with databases and everything in between, also, venturing as far as doing the design.
